Tri Brothers LLC Privacy Policy

Protecting your private information is our priority. This Statement of Privacy applies to the www.tribrothersusa.com website and governs data collection and usage. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, unless otherwise noted, all references to Tri Brothers LLC include www.tribrothersusa.com. The Tri Brothers website is a trucking services platform. By using the Tri Brothers website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.


What is Personal Data?

"Personal Data" is any information that can reasonably be used to identify an individual or otherwise make an individual identifiable. This may include your name, address, email address, phone number, login information (such as account number and password), or payment card number. The types of Personal Data we may process depend on the business context and purposes for which it was collected. It may include:

  • Contact, subscription, and account/registration details, as well as online identifiers.
  • Login credentials where an account is required.
  • Communications content when you interact with Tri Brothers.
  • Financial information (e.g., invoices, payment details).
  • Details related to your business or service requirements.
  • System usage information such as device identifiers or IP addresses.

    • If we link other data with your Personal Data, we will treat that linked data as Personal Data.


    Collection of Your Personal Information

    Tri Brothers LLC may collect personally identifiable information, such as your name, phone number, and email address. Additional personal or non-personal information may be gathered automatically, such as:

  • IP address, browser type, domain names, access times, and referring website addresses.

    • This data is used to operate the service, maintain quality, and provide general usage statistics. Tri Brothers encourages you to review the privacy policies of third-party websites you choose to link to from www.tribrothersusa.com. Tri Brothers is not responsible for the privacy policies or content on external sites.


    Use of Your Personal Information

    Tri Brothers LLC collects and uses your personal information to:

  • Operate its website and deliver the services you request.
  • Provide a secure and optimized user experience.
  • Communicate important updates and promotional offers.

    • We may also collect data from trusted third-party sources and use it to improve our services. Tri Brothers LLC does not sell, rent, or lease customer lists to third parties. However, we may share data with trusted partners for specific purposes, such as statistical analysis or customer support, while maintaining confidentiality.


    Deletion of Your Personal Information

    You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. To initiate this process, please contact us at info@tribrothersusa.com.


    Use of Cookies

    The Tri Brothers website may use cookies to personalize your online experience. Cookies help enhance your interaction by saving time and customizing content based on your preferences. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work properly if cookies are declined.


    Security of Your Personal Information

    Tri Brothers secures your data against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure through robust security measures.


    Children Under Thirteen

    Tri Brothers LLC does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under thirteen. If you are under thirteen, you must obtain permission from a parent or guardian to use this website.
